What does this search?

Hundreds of millions of items including:

  • Books (electronic and print)
  • Journals, magazines, and newspapers (electronic and print)
  • Articles (peer-reviewed and scholarly, as well as reviews, editorials, and letters to the editor) from databases like ERIC, MEDLINE, JSTOR, etc.
  • Music, videos, DVDs, scores
  • Maps, archival, and digital collections
  • Government documents and research datasets
What doesn't this search?

  • Digital Repository for U of M (DRUM)
  • University Digital Conservancy (UDC)
